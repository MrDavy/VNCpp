VNCpp

VNCpp is a free software, it's a VNC viewer to Android build with NDK and powered by LibVNCServer.

Requirements

This software has been built for Android 4.0 or later

Download

You can download the app from google play.

the link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=es.farfuteam.vncpp.controller&hl=es_419

Or you can use the apk inside github.
